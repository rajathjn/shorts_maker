# 🎬 ShortsMaker Video Script: "How It Works"

This document serves as the **official script** for the "How ShortsMaker Works" video. Use this to guide your voice-over and on-screen visuals.

---

## 📽️ Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:10)
**On-Screen Visual:** A collage of viral YouTube Shorts and TikToks.
**Script:**
> "Ever wondered how those viral storytelling shorts are made? You know, the ones with Reddit stories and Minecraft gameplay? Today, we’re showing you how **ShortsMaker** does all the heavy lifting for you!"

---

## 📽️ Scene 2: The Story (0:10 - 0:25)
**On-Screen Visual:** A quick scroll through a Reddit thread (e.g., r/AmITheAsshole or r/AskReddit).
**Script:**
> "First, we need a hook. ShortsMaker automatically heads over to Reddit to find the most engaging stories that people are talking about right now. It picks the best post and grabs the text, ready for transformation."

---

## 📽️ Scene 3: The Script & Voice (0:25 - 0:45)
**On-Screen Visual:** Text on screen transforming from messy to clean. A microphone icon popping up.
**Script:**
> "But a raw post isn't a script. Our tool cleans up the grammar, fixes weird typos, and prepares it for a voice-over. Then, we use high-quality, TikTok-style voices to read the story. It sounds natural, clear, and ready for your ears!"

---

## 📽️ Scene 4: Timing is Everything (0:45 - 1:00)
**On-Screen Visual:** Subtitles appearing word-by-word perfectly synced to a voice (can be mock audio).
**Script:**
> "Timing is everything in a short. We create a detailed transcript that marks exactly when every word is spoken. This ensures those satisfying subtitles pop up right on time, keeping your viewers glued to the screen."

---

## 📽️ Scene 5: Visuals & Music (1:00 - 1:15)
**On-Screen Visual:** Minecraft parkour on the left, satisfying sand-cutting on the right. Musical notes in the middle.
**Script:**
> "Next, we add the eye candy! We pull in exciting background videos like Minecraft parkour or satisfying ASMR. Throw in some low-key background music, and the vibe is set."

---

## 📽️ Scene 6: The Export (1:15 - 1:30)
**On-Screen Visual:** A progress bar hitting 100% and a final MP4 file appearing.
**Script:**
> "Finally, ShortsMaker puts it all together—the voice, the music, the background, and the subtitles. In just a few minutes, you’ve got a high-quality video ready for YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram. That's the power of ShortsMaker!"

---

## 📽️ Scene 7: Outro (1:30 - 1:40)
**On-Screen Visual:** ShortsMaker logo and a 'Get Started' button.
**Script:**
> "Ready to start your own channel? Check out the guide in the assets folder and let’s start creating. Don't forget to like and subscribe for more tips!"

---

### 💡 Tips for the Editor:
- **Fast-Paced:** Keep the cuts quick to match the "Shorts" style.
- **Vibrant Colors:** Use the primary orange (#ff4500) for highlights.
- **Captions:** Always include the word-by-word captions as they are the most important part of the video style.
