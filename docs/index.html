ShortsMaker: How It Works! 🚀

Creating awesome videos from stories you love.

1

Find a Great Story 📖

The first step is finding an amazing story. ShortsMaker looks at Reddit—a place where millions of people share their experiences. It picks a popular story that's perfect for a video!

[Image: A Reddit logo and a magnifying glass searching for a story]
2

Polish the Script ✍️

Before reading it out loud, the tool cleans up the text. It fixes grammar, removes weird characters, and makes sure the "internet slang" sounds right when spoken by our voice artist.

[Image: A pencil and paper turning messy text into clear sentences]
3

Give it a Voice 🎙️

Now for the magic! We use TikTok-style voices to read the script. It's not just a robot; it's a clear, engaging voice that brings the story to life.

[Image: A microphone with sound waves coming out]
4

Write Down the Words 📝

Every single word is timed perfectly. We create a transcript that tells the video editor exactly when each word is spoken so they appear on screen at just the right moment.

[Image: A list of words with timers next to them (0:01 - "Once", 0:02 - "upon")]
5

Pick the Background 🎮

Nobody wants to watch a blank screen. We add Minecraft parkour or satisfying gameplay in the background to keep everyone watching!

Example of the final background with captions

6

Add Some Beats 🎵

Finally, we add some low-key background music. It fills the gaps and makes the whole video feel professional and exciting.

Sample background music

7

The Grand Reveal! 🎉

The computer puts everything together into one MP4 file. Your short is ready to be uploaded to YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram!

[Image: A "Loading" bar finishing and showing a "Download Complete" checkmark]
